How to Start Your Law Firm in Lahore

If you want to open your own law office in Lahore, the first important step is choosing the right legal structure. In Pakistan, lawyers can register their firm as a sole proprietorship, partnership, or private limited company. Here are a few simple steps to get started: Choose your legal structure – Decide which type of entity best fits your firm. Registration and licenses – Register your firm with SECP or the relevant authority and obtain a business license. Taxes and bank account – Get registered with FBR for tax purposes and open a business bank account. Set up your office – Choose a suitable location and start operations.

Frequently Asked Questions

Below are some of the most common questions our clients ask. These FAQs cover matters ranging from starting a legal process to handling complex legal and commercial transactions you may face as your business or case progresses. If you don’t find your question here, feel free to contact our team. We are here to listen, guide you, and help you move forward with confidence.

Why should I hire a business attorney for my startup?

+

Hiring a business attorney for your startup helps you avoid costly legal mistakes from the very beginning. From choosing the right business structure to protecting you from personal liability, handling registrations, contracts, and compliance, an attorney ensures your business is legally secure and prepared for growth. We guide you in making informed decisions that support your long-term success.

What legal structure is best for my business?

+

The best legal structure for your business depends on factors such as liability protection, taxation, business size, and future growth plans. Common options include sole proprietorships, partnerships, and private limited companies. We assess your specific needs and guide you in selecting the most suitable structure while also handling registration, documentation, and legal compliance.

How do you assist with funding and investments?

+

We assist startups in navigating funding and investment processes by providing legal guidance during funding rounds, drafting and reviewing investment agreements, and advising on negotiations with investors. Our support ensures your business is legally protected and positioned for growth while securing the necessary capital.

What ARE General Counsel Services?

+

General Counsel services provide ongoing legal support for your business, including reviewing and drafting contracts, ensuring compliance with laws and regulations, managing legal risks, and offering strategic advice on corporate decisions. Essentially, we act as your in-house legal team to help your business operate safely and efficiently.

How do you protect intellectual property?

+

We protect your intellectual property by helping you register trademarks, copyrights, and patents, as well as drafting and reviewing contracts such as NDAs, licensing agreements, and partnership agreements. Our legal support ensures your creations, brands, and innovations are safeguarded against unauthorized use or infringement.

Do I need a lawyer when buying or selling a business?

+

Yes, hiring a lawyer is highly recommended when buying or selling a business. We help you review and negotiate purchase agreements, conduct due diligence, identify potential liabilities, and ensure the transaction complies with all legal requirements. Our goal is to protect your interests and make the process smooth and secure.

How can you help resolve business disputes?

+

We help resolve business disputes by providing legal advice, negotiating settlements, drafting agreements, and representing your business in mediation, arbitration, or court proceedings if necessary. Our goal is to protect your interests and achieve a fair and efficient resolution while minimizing risk and disruption to your business.
