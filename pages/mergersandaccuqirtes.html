MERGERS AND
ACQUISITIONS

When you're ready to grow your business or sell it, having the right legal support can make all the difference for your success. At Bend Law Group, we help businesses get through mergers and acquisitions (M&A) smoothly. Whether you're acquiring a company, merging with another business or selling your own, our attorneys can guide you every step of the way to make sure the process is efficient and your interests are protected.

M&A for Small and Growing California Businesses

We focus on providing tailored legal services to small businesses. We believe in giving personal attention to every client. When you work with us, you get more than just a lawyer—you get a trusted partner who will support your goals. And if we aren't the right fit for your needs, we'll connect you with someone who is. Let's work together to help your business grow. Whether you're negotiating the purchase of a competitor, merging with another company to expand your reach or selling your business to move on to the next chapter, we're here to ensure you get the best possible outcome.

Our Mergers & Acquisitions Services:

  • Deal Structuring: We can help you decide the best way to structure your deal, whether it's an asset purchase, stock purchase or merger. Our goal is to minimize your risk and maximize value.
  • Due Diligence: We carefully review all aspects of the transaction, making sure there are no hidden liabilities or issues that could create problems down the road. Afterall, sometimes no deal is the best deal.
  • Contract Drafting and Negotiation: From the initial term sheet to the final purchase agreement, we draft and negotiate contracts legal structure for your business, whether it's an LLC, corporation or a sole proprietorship.
  • Regulatory Issues: Failing to comply with regulations, whether it's in setting up your business or in your daily operations, could lead to fines or shutdowns.

Seamless Post-Transaction Support

The work doesn't stop once the deal is closed. After the merger or acquisition, there may be post-transaction tasks that require legal attention, such as transitioning employees, integrating new systems or handling ongoing compliance. We offer continued legal support to make sure the transition is as smooth as possible. Whether it's managing the details of integration or addressing any issues that arise after the transaction, we're here to ensure that your business thrives long after the deal is finalized.

A Small Firm for Your Big Dreams

At Bend Law Group, we intentionally keep our firm small to give you the personal attention your business deserves. Unlike larger firms where your project can get lost in the shuffle, we focus on providing dedicated, one-on-one support for every client. Our boutique size enables us to build closer relationships and gain a deeper understanding of the unique details of your business. When you work with us, you're not just another file - you're a priority.

Why Legal Support for M&A is Critical

Without proper legal guidance, a merger or acquisition can become a legal and financial headache. For example, you might miss important details in the contract that put your company at risk or you could end up with unexpected liabilities from the business you're acquiring. At Bend Law Group, we make sure that every part of the process is thoroughly reviewed, so you don't run into surprises after the deal is done.
