OUR TEAM

Behwal Asad Rasul

Managing Attorney | Strategic Legal Counsel for Entrepreneurs

Behwal Asad Rasul is a distinguished legal professional and a high-achieving Advocate of the High Court. With a deep passion for the law and a commitment to excellence, he has established himself as a trusted advisor for entrepreneurs and businesses navigating complex legal landscapes.

His educational journey is a testament to his academic brilliance. Behwal was an exceptional student, completing his legal education at the prestigious University of London. His academic background is truly international, spanning both Lahore and London, providing him with a unique global perspective on legal challenges.

As the managing force at Bar Law Group, Behwal combines his rigorous legal training with a practical, client-focused approach. Whether representing clients in the High Court or advising startups on formation and growth, he brings a level of clarity and strategic insight that is rare in the field.

Clients trust Behwal Asad Rasul not just for his extensive legal knowledge, but also for his unwavering integrity and dedication. He believes in building long-term partnerships with his clients, ensuring their interests are protected at every turn.
